The difference between Margin, Border and Padding.

Example.

In the example above, margin, border, and padding can be clearly seen.
Margin is the space between the thing you are editing, and the things on the page next to it. From the outside of our example box, there is 50pixels (50px) between it and
our underlined heading, and 50px between it and this paragraph. In this example left and right margins vary in size depending on screen size, as their width is set to Auto.
Border is the outside edge of the item you are editing, as can be seen by the dashed orange line.
Padding is the space between the actual content of the item, in this case the text "Example", and the edge of the box, which is shown as the aqua color.
